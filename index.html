🔍 Fraud Detection Rules
🔄 Duplicate Claim Detection
Flags workers submitting multiple claims within 48 hours. Adds +40 to fraud score.
📍 Zone Validation
Verifies the disruption event covers the worker's registered city/zone. No match = +50 score.
💰 Amount Validation
Claim amounts cannot exceed policy coverage limit. Excess = +30 fraud score.
🆕 New Worker Flag
Workers registered less than 7 days ago trigger elevated scrutiny. Adds +20 score.
📡 Location Mismatch (GPS)
GPS coordinates during claim must match registered zone. Mismatch adds +35 score.
Decision Logic: Score <30 → Auto Approved | Score 30–60 → Under Review | Score >60 → Auto Rejected
